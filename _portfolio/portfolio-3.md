title: "Deep learning in porous media"
excerpt: >
  Our project focused on applying deep learning methods to predict the key transport properties of porous media directly from their geometry. We showed that neural-network models can infer transport coefficients and reconstruct concentration fields directly from porous structures, providing efficient surrogate models for complex transport phenomena.
