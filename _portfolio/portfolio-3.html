--- title: "Deep learning in porous media" excerpt: "In paper Sci. Rep 10, 21488 (2020), we proved the deep learning systems' ability to encode information about porous media fluid dynamics. We adapted the convolutional neural networks (CNN) to relate initial configurations of obstacles, represented by a picture, with three fundamental quantities in the porous media: porosity, permeability, and tortuosity. We showed that our CNN reproduces the values of porosity, permeability, and tortuosity with reasonable accuracy.
